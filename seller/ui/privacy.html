PalitOra – Privacy Policy for Sellers (Shop Owners)

Effective Date: January 29, 2026

PalitOra (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal and business information of all registered sellers using our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use PalitOra as a shop owner in Dumaguete City.

By registering as a seller on PalitOra, you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

When you register and operate as a seller on PalitOra, we may collect the following information:

1.1 Personal Information

  • Full name
  • Contact number
  • Email address
  • Valid identification (if required for verification)

1.2 Business Information

  • Shop name
  • Business address/location
  • Product listings and pricing
  • Business permits or licenses (if applicable)

1.3 Transaction Data

  • Order details and history
  • Payment and settlement records
  • Customer interactions related to orders

1.4 Technical Data

  • Device information
  • IP address
  • App usage data and activity logs

2. How We Use Your Information

We use seller information to:

  • Create and manage your seller account
  • Display your shop and products to customers
  • Process and manage orders and deliveries
  • Facilitate payments and settlements
  • Communicate important updates and notifications
  • Improve platform performance and user experience
  • Ensure compliance with platform policies and legal obligations

3. Sharing of Information

PalitOra may share your information only when necessary:

3.1 With Customers

  • Shop name
  • Product details
  • Business location (for delivery purposes)

3.2 With Riders

  • Order details
  • Pickup location
  • Contact information (limited to delivery coordination)

3.3 With Service Providers

  • Payment processors
  • Cloud storage and system infrastructure providers

3.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or legal process in the Philippines.

We do NOT sell your personal or business data to third parties.

4. Data Protection and Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Secure servers and encrypted data transmission
  • Access control and authentication systems
  • Regular monitoring for unauthorized access

However, no system is completely secure. Sellers are responsible for safeguarding their account credentials.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information:

  • As long as your account is active
  • As necessary for legal, accounting, or operational purposes

You may request account deletion, but certain records (e.g., transactions) may be retained as required by law.

6. Seller Responsibilities

As a seller, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate and updated information
  • Protect customer data you receive through the platform
  • Use information only for order fulfillment purposes
  • Avoid misuse, sharing, or unauthorized disclosure of customer data

Violation of these responsibilities may result in account suspension or termination.

7. Your Rights (Under Philippine Data Privacy Laws)

As a data subject, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal limitations)
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

Requests may be submitted through PalitOra support channels.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

PalitOra may use cookies or similar technologies to:

  • Enhance user experience
  • Analyze platform usage
  • Improve system performance

You may control these through your device settings.

10. Updates to This Policy

PalitOra reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Updates will be communicated through the app or official channels.

Continued use of the platform indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

By registering as a seller on PalitOra, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Privacy Policy.
